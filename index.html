This App helps you visualize how water disperses when a water ballon is popped using arrays.
The concentration of the water reduces progressively with distance from the ballon. This is how it works:

  • Numbers reduce progressively from both right and left directions i.e 3,0,0,0
  • ensure you enter positive integers like so; 0,0,2,0,0
  • ensure you avoid strings
